MeetMe!


This project is to help users meet up via their google calanders


Security Info: This app will ask you to authinticate with your google account. It will then pull your calender information, ask you if you wish to ignore certain events, then will only log your free/busy times. It WILL NOT save your event titles, and will only track free/busy times withen the given range. Whenever this app will potentially save data, the user will be notified.
